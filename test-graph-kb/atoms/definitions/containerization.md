**Containerization** encapsulates an application with its environment and dependencies.

Benefits:
- Consistent runtime across environments
- Lightweight compared to VMs
- Easy deployment via [[atoms/methods/docker-container-setup]]
- Foundation for [[atoms/definitions/kubernetes]]
