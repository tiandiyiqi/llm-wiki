**Authentication** verifies who a user is.

Common methods:
- Password-based
- OAuth/OIDC (see [[atoms/methods/oauth-integration]])
- JWT tokens ([[atoms/facts/jwt-security]])
- Multi-factor authentication

Critical for [[atoms/definitions/web-application]] security.
