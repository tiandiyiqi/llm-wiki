**Microservices Architecture** breaks applications into small, loosely coupled services.

Key characteristics:
- Each service handles a specific business capability
- Services communicate via APIs (see [[atoms/definitions/rest-api]])
- Independent deployment and scaling
- Often uses [[atoms/definitions/containerization]]
