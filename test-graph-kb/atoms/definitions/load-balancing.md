**Load Balancing** improves availability and performance.

Strategies:
- Round-robin
- Least connections
- IP hash

Tools: Nginx, HAProxy (see [[atoms/methods/nginx-load-balancer]])

Essential for [[atoms/definitions/microservices]] scaling.
